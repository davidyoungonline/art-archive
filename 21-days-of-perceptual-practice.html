21 Days of Perceptual Practice

David Young
Audio series — August 2020

What if perception — the very way we see and experience the world — was something that we could decide and change? What if perception were a lever for creating freedom and movement in one’s life?

Brandenburg 2020

Over three weeks in August 2020, I sent a daily audio meditation and a short practical exercise to a group of practitioners around the world — delivered each evening by WhatsApp. Each audio runs around twelve minutes and is designed for lying down, on the floor or a bed, with a book under your head and your knees bent toward the ceiling. You will need a notebook.

The series moves through three weeks: the first grounded in Alexander Technique and the body’s perceptual habits; the second opening outward through art, movement and the work of Deborah Hay; the third turning toward stillness, chance and the Buddhist tradition.
