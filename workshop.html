Sky Island Lab

A week-long creative lab on the island of Andros, Greece, in October 2026. Dates to be confirmed.

Sky Island Lab is an invitation for a small group of experienced artists and performers to meet and explore together — in the studio and in the open air of ancient Palaiopoli and its valley.

· · ·

About

Hellen Sky is a choreographer, performer and digital artist whose practice spans dance, technology and live performance. She brings a unique deep attentiveness and openness to her arts practice. She will lead the work.

· · ·

Particulars

  • Where Palaiopoli, Andros, Cyclades, Greece
  • When October 2026 — dates to be confirmed
  • Duration One week
  • Open to Experienced artists and performers, all disciplines
  • Fee By donation
  • Accom Self-organised — we can assist with selecting from the limited range of options in the valley
  • Language English
