A Leaf from the Book of Cities

David Young, 2006
for clarinet, violin, viola, violoncello, tuba, pianoforte & percussion
Commissioned by the Ives Ensemble, Amsterdam
with the support of Arts Victoria

Two movements, each taking its title from a Paul Klee painting held in the Öffentliche Kunstsammlung Basel. Klee's titles are among the strangest and most accurate acts of naming in twentieth-century art. Borrowing two of them felt less like homage than like permission.

I. A Leaf from the Book of Cities — after Ein Blatt aus dem Städtebuch, 1928
II. Cooling in a Garden of the Torrid Zone — after ARA "Kühlung in einem Garten der heissen Zone", 1924

Duration: circa 8 minutes.

· · ·

The source paintings

Paul Klee, Ein Blatt aus dem Städtebuch, 1928, and ARA "Kühlung in einem Garten der heissen Zone", 1924.
Both: tempera, oil and watercolour on paper. Öffentliche Kunstsammlung Basel.
